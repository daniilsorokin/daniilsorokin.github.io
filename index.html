Daniil Sorokin

I am a PhD researcher in Natural Language Processing at the UKP Lab, Technische Universität Darmstadt and I work on linking texts to knowledge bases. I have recently published on the topics of relation extraction and semantic parsing for question answering. I am in the final year of my PhD studies with the approximate submission date in June 2019.

Before I started the PhD program, I have completed a master degree in NLP at the University of Tübingen and had an experience working at a machine translation company.

From November 2018 to January 2019, I am interning at Factmata and working on evidence-based claim verification.

I have published in EMNLP, COLING, ESWC, *SEM and other venues. I was a PC member of/reviewer for ACL 2018, CONLL 2018, EMNLP 2018.
